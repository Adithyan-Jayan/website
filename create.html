Create

The Create pathway supports students, founders, researchers, and startups to transform early ideas into practical innovation opportunities.

It focuses on idea generation, prototyping, problem exploration, and early-stage startup development within the JCU innovation ecosystem.

What this supports

  • Developing startup ideas and concepts
  • Building prototypes and proof-of-concept models
  • Exploring innovation opportunities with JCU support
  • Preparing ideas for future commercialisation
