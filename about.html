About JCU Founders-in-Residence Commercialisation

A professional innovation and commercialisation platform connecting student founders, startups, mentors, investors, researchers, and industry partners through the JCU innovation ecosystem.

ABOUT THE PLATFORM

Supporting founders, startups and industry collaboration

JCU Founders-in-Residence Commercialisation is designed to support entrepreneurship, innovation development, startup collaboration, mentoring, and business growth opportunities within the James Cook University community.

The platform helps transform ideas into real-world projects, prototypes, startups, and commercial opportunities while building strong connections between students, researchers, founders, investors, mentors, and industry partners.

Create

Support for new ideas, prototypes, startup concepts, and innovation projects.

Connect

Access to mentors, students, researchers, investors, alumni, and industry networks.

Commercialise

Pathways to business growth, venture development, and commercial outcomes.

What members can access

  • Startup and founder support
  • Industry collaboration opportunities
  • Innovation workspaces and meeting rooms
  • AI commercialisation tools
  • Mentoring and investor engagement
  • Student internship collaboration
  • Media, presentation, and prototyping facilities
