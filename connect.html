Connect

The Connect pathway helps founders and industry partners build meaningful relationships across the JCU innovation community.

It supports networking with mentors, researchers, alumni, investors, students, and industry professionals who can contribute to startup and commercialisation outcomes.

What this supports

  • Connecting with mentors and industry partners
  • Accessing JCU networks and alumni opportunities
  • Building investor and founder relationships
  • Supporting collaboration between students, startups, and industry
